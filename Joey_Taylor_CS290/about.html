Our Mission

We're passionate Marvel Snap players ourselves, and we noticed the lack of a simple tool for players to share and track their deck collections. Our mission is to provide an easy-to-use platform for Marvel Snap enthusiasts to manage their card collections, build decks, and share their setups with the community.

What We Offer

Our website features include:

  1. A comprehensive database of Marvel Snap cards.
  2. A personal collection tracker to monitor your card acquisition progress.
  3. A deck builder that helps you craft and share your perfect deck.

The Team

Our team is made up of dedicated Marvel and digital card game fans. We're developers, designers, and, most importantly, players like you -- By that I mean I am just one guy working on this for a school Computer Science project. However, we're committed to improving the Marvel Snap Collection Tracker and keeping it up-to-date with the game's latest expansions and updates.
